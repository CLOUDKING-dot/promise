About Us

God's Promise of Heaven Charity Foundation Uganda is committed to transforming lives in underprivileged communities across Uganda through impactful initiatives in education, healthcare, and sustainable development. Established in 2010, our mission is to foster hope, empower individuals, and build resilient communities.

We provide access to quality education through scholarships and school infrastructure, deliver essential healthcare services via medical camps and health education, and promote economic empowerment through vocational training and community development projects. Our community-driven approach ensures sustainable change, empowering individuals and families to achieve self-reliance and dignity. Join us in our mission to make a lasting impact.

Our Services

  • Education Support

    We provide scholarships, school supplies, and modern infrastructure to ensure every child has access to quality education, paving the way for a brighter future.

  • Healthcare Services

    Our medical camps and health education programs deliver critical healthcare services and promote wellness in underserved communities.

  • Livelihood Programs

    Through vocational training and small business support, we empower individuals to achieve economic independence and build sustainable livelihoods.

  • Community Development

    We build sustainable infrastructure, such as water wells and community centers, to enhance living conditions and foster community growth.

Education Programs

  1. Scholarship Program

    2010 — Present

    Our scholarship program provides full and partial funding to underprivileged students, enabling them to access quality education and unlock their potential.

Healthcare Programs

  1. Mobile Medical Camps

    2015 — Present

    Our mobile medical camps bring free healthcare services, including check-ups and treatments, to remote communities with limited access to medical facilities.

  2. Health Education

    2016 — Present

    We conduct workshops on hygiene, nutrition, and disease prevention to empower communities with knowledge for healthier living.
